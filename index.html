Crystal Game

You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. If you go over, you lose.

The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
